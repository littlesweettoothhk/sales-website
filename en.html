Our Sweet Story

"Little Sweet Tooth" was founded in 2024. We have a big dream in our hearts - to make desserts so delicious they make people smile, and each one has its own cute little story!

Every dessert has been repeatedly adjusted just to present the most perfect texture and flavor layers.

We make every dessert with a craftsman's heart, hoping that the moment you taste it, the corners of your mouth will involuntarily rise, worries will be temporarily set aside, and you will enjoy pure sweet joy.
