Classic French Pastry

Dacquoise

HKD$40.00

A classic dessert originating from Dax in southwestern France. Our Dacquoise is handcrafted using premium almond flour and egg whites, baked at low temperatures to create a meringue that is light and crispy on the outside and cloud-soft on the inside.


Sandwiched with our signature rich buttercream filling, it offers distinct layers of texture that are sweet but not overwhelming. Every bite delivers the delicate aroma of almonds and the silkiness of cream.
